How it works

1
🔍 Browse shows
Find an open competition and pick the classes that match your pet.
2
📋 Submit your entry
Fill in your details and upload a photo of your pet via our entry form. Each class costs €5.
3
💳 Pay securely
Pay by card (Stripe) or MobilePay. You'll receive a confirmation email.
4
🏆 Win rosettes
Results are judged by licensed professionals. Winners receive a real rosette posted to their door.

Entry fee is €5 per class. No hidden costs.
