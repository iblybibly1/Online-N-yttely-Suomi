Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enter a show?

Browse open competitions, click "Enter this class", fill in the entry form with your details and pet photo, then pay at checkout.

What does it cost?

€5 per class entry. There are no hidden fees.

When will I get results?

Results are usually published within 2 weeks of the closing date. You'll be notified by email.

Can I enter multiple classes?

Yes! Add as many classes as you like to your cart and pay once.

How are rosettes delivered?

Winners receive a real physical rosette posted directly to their door. Make sure your postal address is correct when entering.

What photo should I upload?

A clear, well-lit photo of your pet. The animal should be the main subject. No filters please.

Who judges the entries?

All classes are judged by licensed professionals.

How do I contact you?

Email us at info@onlineshowsuomi.fi
