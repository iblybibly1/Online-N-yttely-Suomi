Payment Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

Entry Fees

All entry fees are charged in Euros (EUR) and are payable in full at the time of entry submission. The entry fee per class is €5.00, with the exception of the Grand Prix — Best in Show class which carries an entry fee of €10.00. Fees are subject to change at the sole discretion of Online Show Suomi; any changes will take effect for future competitions and will not apply retroactively to confirmed entries.

No Refund Policy

All entry fees are strictly non-refundable. By submitting a competition entry and completing payment, the competitor acknowledges and agrees that no refund will be issued under any circumstances, including but not limited to: withdrawal from the competition after payment; failure to submit a qualifying photograph before the closing date; disqualification of an entry for breach of competition rules; technical difficulties experienced by the competitor when attempting to submit an entry; dissatisfaction with judging outcomes or placements; illness or unavailability of the competitor or their animal; cancellation of an entry by the competitor for any reason whatsoever.

Disqualified or Rejected Entries

Online Show Suomi reserves the right to disqualify or reject any entry that does not comply with the competition rules, photo guidelines, or these terms. In the event that an entry is disqualified or rejected — whether before or after judging — no refund of the entry fee will be made. The competitor will be notified by email if their entry has been rejected, with a brief explanation where possible.

Show Cancellation by Online Show Suomi

In the exceptional circumstance that Online Show Suomi cancels an entire competition show before judging has commenced, affected entrants will be offered a credit equal to their entry fee, redeemable against a future competition within 12 months. Credits are non-transferable, have no cash value, and will not be issued if the competitor has already received their judging feedback or result. Online Show Suomi reserves the right to postpone, modify, merge, or cancel individual classes or entire shows without liability beyond the credit described in this clause.

Payment Processing

Payments are processed securely via Stripe or MobilePay. Online Show Suomi does not store payment card details. The competitor is responsible for ensuring that sufficient funds are available and that the payment is completed successfully. An entry is not confirmed until payment has been received in full. Online Show Suomi accepts no liability for failed or duplicate payments caused by third-party payment processors; competitors experiencing payment issues should contact their bank or card provider in the first instance.

Duplicate Entries

If a competitor submits and pays for the same class more than once in error, only one entry will be accepted for judging. The duplicate entry fee will not be refunded. Competitors are advised to review their cart carefully before completing payment.

Chargebacks and Disputes

Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or card provider in respect of a valid entry fee constitutes a breach of these terms. Online Show Suomi reserves the right to permanently ban any competitor who initiates a fraudulent or unjustified chargeback from participating in future competitions. Where a chargeback is successfully reversed by the payment processor, Online Show Suomi reserves the right to pursue recovery of the disputed amount through appropriate legal channels.

Prize Payments

Where a cash prize is awarded (such as the Grand Prix €50 prize), Online Show Suomi will contact the winner by email within 14 days of results being published to arrange payment. Prize payments are made via bank transfer or PayPal at Online Show Suomi's discretion. Prizes unclaimed within 60 days of the winner being notified will be forfeited. Online Show Suomi is not responsible for incorrect payment details provided by the winner.

Currency and Taxes

All fees and prizes are denominated in Euros (EUR). Online Show Suomi is not responsible for any currency conversion fees, foreign transaction fees, or tax obligations that may apply to the competitor in their jurisdiction. Entry fees may be subject to Finnish VAT where applicable.

Amendments to This Policy

Online Show Suomi reserves the right to amend this Payment Policy at any time. The current version will always be published on this page. Continued use of the platform following any amendment constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. Material changes will be communicated via the website.

Contact

For payment queries, email info@onlineshowsuomi.fi. Please include your full name, competition name, and payment reference. We aim to respond within 3 business days.
